Annexure – II

GUIDELINES, TERMS & CONDITIONS (for the utilization of VGST grant)

GUIDELINES

  1. The VGST grant released under CESEM/ CISEE/ K-FIST L1 & L2/ RGS/F schemes are for strengthening Science and Technology infrastructure facilities of the identified Department in Universities/Colleges and other Higher Educational Institutions for any other objective such as promoting quality teaching and research. The VGST grant shall not be utilized for the construction of rooms, buildings, appointment of supporting staff and any such other expenditures etc.
  2. The Faculty member who applies for a VGST grant will be designated as the “Principal Investigator”, with a compulsory provision for naming a Co-Principal Investigator.
  3. Grantee Institution has to constitute a “Purchase Committee” which includes Head of the Institution as Chairman of the Purchase Committee with four additional members as indicated (Sl.No.28). The Purchase Committee is authorized to procure only VGST approved equipment/ items as indicated in the Budget Estimate of Grant Related Document (GRD).
  4. All issues, matters & procedures related to the procurement of equipment/ items etc. shall be recorded in the “Proceedings of the Purchase Committee” duly signed by all the members of the purchase committee. There shall not be any deviation in purchase/ procurement of the Equipment/ items/listed in the proposal and from the Approved Cost prescribed in the VGST approval of the project.
  5. Appropriation of approved recurring expenditure to the non-recurring category, and vice-versa, is not permitted unless approved by VGST.
  6. The grantee Institution may procure equipment and its auxiliaries taking them as one unit under procurement.
  7. During any appropriation process, the cost/ price of an item shall not exceed 10% to 15% of the cost of the equipment item as approved by the VGST. Any further deviation is strictly not allowed.
  8. Grants released under the VGST scheme need to be kept in a savings bank account of a Nationalized Bank. The account shall be opened in the name of drawees designation. The accrued interest on the grant needs to be reflected in Financial Status Proforma (FSP) annually.
  9. Grantee Institution shall procure only those equipments/ items for which VGST’s approval is accorded in the Budget Estimate (both Non-Recurring & Recurring). Any deviation from this will lead to withdrawal of the VGST Grant and no further grant will be released, to the PI or the Institution.
  10. Grantee institution shall strictly adhere to follow the percentage of the grant allotted to Expenditure sanctioned and approved by VGST namely Non-Recurring expenditures such as Equipment, Software etc (maximum 80% or 90%) and Recurring expenditures such as Chemicals, Contingency, Books, Journals etc. (maximum 20% or 10%).
  11. Interest amount earned from the VGST grants at the end of the Financial year cannot be utilized without the prior approval of VGST.
  12. All the assets acquired from the VGST grants will be the property of the Department of IT, BT and S & T, Government of Karnataka. A register of the Recurring and Non-recurring assets acquired wholly or partly out of this grant shall be maintained and a copy thereof furnished to this Department. Such a register / ledger of assets and accounts maintained shall be available or open to scrutiny by Competent authorities in the Government.
  13. n order to give visibility and publicity to VGST schemes, viz, CESEM/ CISEE/ K-FIST L1 & L2/ RGS/F, the respective Bonafide Department should acknowledge and display a name board/sticker containing the Name of the equipment, name of the scheme under which the sanctioned is made and the Grantee agency as Vision Group on Science and Technology, Department of Science and Technology, Govt. of Karnataka.
  14. After VGST grant utilization, the grantee Institution shall furnish the Progress Report, the Utilization Certificate (UC) and the Statement of Expenditure (SE) to VGST without fail, and provide an attested photocopy of the bank pass book
  15. Release of 2nd or 3rd instalment of VGST grants, if any, is considered only after receiving the Utilization Certificate and Statement of Expenditure (SE) and progress report of the project, in the prescribed format followed by review of the project by the subcommittee of VGST.
  16. After the recommendation of the Review Committee, the unutilized grant with accrued interest at the end of the project phase shall be carried forward (if the program is continued) or be refunded (if the program is completed), whichever is applicable by making appropriate entries in Financial Status Proforma.
  17. Karnataka Science and Technology Promotion Society (KSTePS)/VGST/ authorized auditor /Auditor General-Karnataka, shall have, at his discretion, the right to access the book of accounts and ledgers of the VGST Scheme (CESEM/ CISEE/ K-FIST L1 & L2/ RGS/F and FDP whichever is applicable). VGST, DST, GoK may also nominate a CAG empanelled Auditor for the Audit of accounts of the VGST programme or Project granted to the institution/Co-ordinator.
  18. After the completion of the VGST project/programme, the unspent grant with accrued interest if any shall be returned to VGST by Cheque or DD drawn in favor of “Managing Director, KSTePS, Bengaluru”.
  19. The Programme Progress Evaluation Committee (PPEC) may visit the College/ Institution at any time as it feels necessary to monitor the project.
  20. You may contact VGST office for any enquiries / clarifications. (Email: visiongroup.st@gmail.com, Phone: 080 – 2203 2013).
  21. Both grantee institution & the Principal Investigator/ Co-Principal Investigator undertake the responsibility of implementing the sanctioned VGST project completely within the duration fixed by VGST. In case of any delay in the implementation of the project within the stipulated period, the institution should refund/ return the left-over amount with accrued interest to VGST through a cheque or DD in favor of “Managing Director, KSTePS, Bengaluru”.
  22. For all purposes please indicate always Grant Related Document Number (GRD No.)
  23. If any deviation or violation from the approved items/ cost and procurement procedures, VGST reserves the right to cancel the release of the next instalment of grant / ordering for recovery of grant as recommended by the review committee.
  24. The Project (equipment, grant amount) cannot be transferred from one Institution to another Institution under any circumstances. In this regard, no correspondence is entertained and responded.
